隱私權政策
Privacy Policy

KnitNote 的工作資料留在你的裝置上。沒有帳號、廣告、分析或追蹤。

繁體中文

KnitNote 是一款在 iPhone、iPad、Apple Watch 與 Mac 上使用的編織工具。如有問題，請寄信至 lzz.1999@icloud.com。

我們不收集的資料

KnitNote 不需要帳號，不含廣告、分析工具或第三方追蹤 SDK，不會跨 App 或網站追蹤你，也不會把作品、照片、織圖、毛線資料、筆記、日記或備份傳送到開發者營運的伺服器。

裝置上的資料

作品、計數器、織圖閱讀狀態、筆記、手寫標記、日記、毛線庫與設定儲存在你的裝置上。Apple Watch 透過 Apple 的裝置間通訊與配對 iPhone 同步作品和計數器；開發者不會收到內容。

相機、照片與檔案

只有當你主動拍照時才會要求相機權限。照片、PDF、圖片與備份套件只會在你使用系統選擇器挑選後，為你要求的匯入或還原操作而讀取。

備份與還原

只有當你主動匯出時才會建立外部備份。你選擇儲存位置並自行管理；還原只讀取你明確選擇的 KnitNote 備份套件。

購買與 App Store

購買、付款與商店交易由 Apple 處理。KnitNote 不會收到付款卡號或 Apple 帳號密碼；Apple 處理的資料受其政策規範。

保存、刪除與兒童隱私

資料保留在裝置上，直到你在 App 內刪除、移除 App 或清除裝置資料。外部備份由你自行刪除。KnitNote 不刻意收集任何人的個人資料，包括兒童。

政策變更

更新時會在本頁標示新的生效日期；重大變更會隨 App 更新或支援頁面說明。

English

KnitNote is a knitting tool for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac. Questions may be sent to lzz.1999@icloud.com.

Data we do not collect

KnitNote requires no account and includes no advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking SDK. It does not track you across apps or websites and does not transmit projects, photos, patterns, yarn details, notes, journals, or backups to a developer-operated server.

Data on your devices

Projects, counters, pattern-reading state, notes, handwritten marks, journals, yarn inventory, and settings are stored on your device. Apple Watch synchronizes project and counter state with its paired iPhone through Apple's device-to-device communication. The developer does not receive this content.

Camera, photos, and files

KnitNote requests camera access only when you choose to take a photo. Photos, PDFs, images, and backup packages are read only after you select them with a system picker and only for the import or restore operation you requested.

Backup and restore

An external backup package is created only when you explicitly export one. You choose its storage location and manage it. Restore reads only the KnitNote backup package you explicitly select.

Purchase and the App Store

Apple processes app purchases, payments, and store transactions. KnitNote does not receive your payment-card number or Apple Account password. Apple's own policies govern data Apple processes.

Retention, deletion, and children's privacy

Data stays on your device until you delete it in the app, remove the app, or erase device data. You delete exported backups from their chosen location. KnitNote does not intentionally collect personal data from anyone, including children.

Policy changes

The updated effective date will appear here. Material changes will be described with an app update or on the support site.
